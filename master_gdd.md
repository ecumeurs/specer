## 1. THE SHARED UNIVERSE (Narrative & Vibe)
*The "Soul" that remains constant regardless of which app is open.*

### 1.1 The High Concept
> "Survival on a reality-unstable planet where corporate greed, shadow warfare, and frontline survival are one and the same."

### 1.2 Design Pillars (The "Golden Rules")
* **Interdependence:** No app is an island.
* **Friction as Value:** Complexity and loss are part of the progression.
* **Verticality:** Everything moves from the "Wild" (T1) to the "Core" (T10).

---

## 2. THE ECOSYSTEM ECONOMY (The "Flow")
*How value moves between the three separate games.*

### 2.1 Global Resource Map
* **Primary Resource (Matter):** Extracted in *Commerce*, used for gear in *Battle*.
* **Secondary Resource (Intel):** Generated in *Intrigue*, provides buffs in *Battle*.
* **Tertiary Resource (Security):** Generated in *Battle*, lowers "Reality Glitch" rates in *Commerce*.

### 2.2 The Global State Machine
* **Volatility:** When *Battle* players fail, Volatility rises across all apps.
* **Prosperity:** When *Commerce* players thrive, funding for *Intrigue* missions increases.
